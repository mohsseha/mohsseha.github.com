Four Tannery Brook Row, Unit 15

Viewings by appointment only

MLS#: 72831134

 

Bright, Vibrant, & Cheerful Oasis

Unit 15 is an excellent example of loft living with high ceilings, exoskeletal design, and spacious feel. Nine tall windows allow abundant sunshine to flood the unit and make even a grey New England day feel bright and cheerful. European, Middle-Eastern, and Asian finishing touches will inspire lovers of travel and luxury design. 

All the Shiny Things

Your carefully curated kitchen is designed with passionate chefs in mind: six-burner stove, double-drawer dishwasher, instant hot water, and SmartSteel refrigerator. Gone are the days of only one cook in the kitchen. You and your sous chef can glide across the intelligently designed appliances without tripping over each other. The six-burner stove has a lofted plate warmer for those moments when all the dishes aren’t timed just right. Not to be unacknowledged, the unblemished stainless steel cabinetry keeps less glamorous cookware out of sight. 

Find the Solace (and Storage!) You Crave

Though it is a loft, you will still find privacy with a traditional bedroom with a proper door — unusual among our lofts. Moreover, you won’t struggle for places to tuck away your cherished items with the ample storage. You will love the large walk-in closet with handmade shelving using reclaimed wood and brass piping. 

Step into Marrakech

We love the boldness of the blue accent wall saturated in imported lapis lazuli paint — the same paint that adorns the Jardin Majorelle in Marrakech! The ultramarine blue provides a powerful anchor point as it peaks behind a partition into the primary living space. Behind the partition, dwellers will find the perfect study, guest room, or spot for a new housemate. This point of exemplary design continues as we make our way through the unit.

Travel to Italy, Japan, and Anatolia Every Morning

This large, recently renovated bathroom is nothing but a sanctuary for connoisseurs of exquisite design. The vibrancy of the Scavolini glass sink is soothed by the peacefulness of the buttery grey Arabesque tiling in the shower, sleek Italian marble flooring, and rainfall shower head. Have you ever enjoyed a toilet seat customized for your tush? We have, and it is nothing short of sublime. It even knows when you’ve entered the room! Making its way all the way from Japan, this toilet is a smarty pants.

Just a Few More Things

Unit 15 is perched on the third floor of this fantastic treehouse. On one side, you’ll enjoy voyeuristic views of the bike path. On the other, you’ll enjoy your balcony on the fiberglass skywalk and chat with your welcoming neighbors. Building insurance, water, snow removal, trash removal, and landscaping are covered by the condo fee. Additionally, the site benefits from attentive on-site property management.

Come see this beauty!
